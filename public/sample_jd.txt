SENIOR AI/ML ENGINEER — PERSPEX INTELLIGENCE LABS
==================================================

ABOUT THE ROLE
--------------
We are hiring a Senior AI/ML Engineer to join our core research and product team.
You will design, build, and ship production-grade machine learning systems that power
Perspex's candidate-intelligence platform. This is a high-ownership, high-impact role:
you will work across the full ML lifecycle — from research and experimentation to
deployment and monitoring — with direct influence on product direction.

WHAT YOU'LL DO
--------------
- Architect and implement large-scale ML pipelines for embedding, retrieval, and ranking
- Fine-tune and evaluate large language models (LLMs) for domain-specific ranking tasks
- Design and maintain vector search infrastructure (FAISS, Pinecone, Weaviate, or similar)
- Build evaluation frameworks and maintain ground-truth datasets for continuous improvement
- Collaborate with product and engineering to ship ML features end-to-end
- Define and monitor production model health metrics; own the model reliability SLA
- Mentor mid-level engineers; drive best practices in experimentation and model governance
- Contribute to internal tooling for dataset labelling, feature engineering, and A/B testing

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
-----------------------
- 5+ years of industry experience in ML/AI engineering or applied research
- Proficiency in Python; strong command of PyTorch or JAX
- Hands-on experience with transformer-based LLMs (fine-tuning, RLHF, evaluation)
- Experience building semantic search or recommendation systems at scale
- Familiarity with MLOps: experiment tracking (MLflow/W&B), model registries, CI/CD for ML
- Strong understanding of information retrieval, embeddings, and approximate nearest neighbour (ANN) algorithms
- Ability to read and implement ML research papers independently
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
------------------------
- Experience with Gemini, GPT-4, Claude, or other frontier model APIs
- Background in NLP, ranking systems, or talent/HR-tech domains
- Prior work with RLHF, DPO, or other alignment techniques
- Published research (conference papers, pre-prints) in ML/AI
- Experience deploying ML systems on GCP, AWS, or Azure
